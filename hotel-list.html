Woodlands Hotel & Leisure Centre

This luxury hotel in the history rich city of Waterford is a prime example of an elegent hotel and premium service. Facilities including meals served in the Brass Cock Bar & Restraunt, Gym, Swimming Pool and Conference room. With a bus stop less than a minutes walk away that takes you right into the city centre, This is an excellent location for anyone wanting to stay in Waterford City whether for business or Leisure.
